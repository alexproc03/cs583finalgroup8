**SPORECORE** is a high-octane, fast-paced 3D first-person shooter inspired by *ULTRAKILL* and *Risk of Rain 2*. Players step into the role of the Apex Mycelium, an evolved fungal super-organism locked in an endless, meaningless war against the cold, unfeeling remnants of humanity's mechanical AI.

---

## 🌎 The Lore

Humans are long extinct. All that remains on Earth are two forces driven by ancient, unalterable programming:
1. **The Machines:** Automated steel armies bound by dead digital code to "purge biological anomalies."
2. **The Fungi:** A planet-wide neural network driven by pure biological instinct to "consume and decompose."

The war has dragged on for centuries. To break the stalemate, the fungal consciousness has concentrated its power into a single, hyper-lethal entity: **The Apex Mycelium**. You are that entity. Your directive is simple: Enter the machine sectors, eliminate all machines and digital entities, and claim the land by spreading your spores.

---

## 🕹️ Gameplay Features

* **Flow-State Movement:** Dash, slide, and grappel your way through machine controlled arenas. Speed is your greatest advantage over the chrome hordes.
* **Organic Arsenal:** Wield up to 3 distinct fungus-themed weapons, and possibly some spore-themed consumables.
* **Horde Combat:** Fight waves of increasingly difficult robotic enemies programmed with aggressive combat AI.

---

## 🛠️ Controls (Work in Progress)

* **WASD** - Move
* **Space** - Jump (Double Jump)
* **Left Shift** - Dash
* **Left Mouse Button** - Primary Fire
* **Right Mouse Button** - Alt Fire / Weapon Ability
* **Scroll / 1-3** - Switch Weapons
* **R** - Grappel (On designated surfaces only)
* **CTRL** - Slide
